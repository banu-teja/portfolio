Mastering Tailwind CSS

Tailwind CSS has revolutionized the way we approach web design. Let’s dive into some of its key concepts and best practices.

The Utility-First Approach

Tailwind CSS promotes a utility-first methodology, which means:

  • You compose designs directly in your markup
  • You have fine-grained control over your styles
  • Your CSS bundle size remains small, even for large projects

Responsive Design Made Easy

Tailwind makes it simple to create responsive layouts:

{@html `<div class="w-full md:w-1/2 lg:w-1/3">
	<!-- Your content here -->
</div>`}
